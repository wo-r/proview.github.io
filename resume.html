About Me

My coding adventures started sometime around 2019. It originally started as a little joke because of my weird ambitions, making these weird template websites from a service called NicePage. One such thing was this website called Virtia, a "Made For Custom VPN Addresses, Used For Chromebooks As A Way To Block Common Web Blockers" website. Yeaaaaaaa, I have no clue what I was trying to do back then. After that weird start to my programming development, I had actually learn't how to make websites from raw HTML. This sparked projects like Vote-Ralph, Soldier Island Display, and Jxzy (a privated github repo that would attempt to crash your browser). Realising that I was pretty good at adapting to programming, I started to get more ambitious. I eventually found this free web hosting website called 000Webhost, ran and supported by Hostinger. It allowed me to learn SQL and PHP. I made many websites, but unfortunately they were not saved :( Around 2021, I had started to get very annoyed by Echo─a LMS website─ran by Agilix that our school used for school work, and grading. I started to making very complex google extensions like Themius (privated), CustomSkins (privated), Echo-Plus, and recently Gradpass. All attempting to fix and create a better experience using Echo. And with each iteration, my programming experience doubled. It's genuinely been a learning curve at every step, which I have pushed through time and time again. Its now 2024 and I have experience is just about any frontend language, and could possibly learn more backend if needed. I'm very flexible, and very capable. I can figure out a problem, and solve it in a relativitly managable time. And in general, I just love coding. I will always do it, no matter how complex or frustrating it is.
