Portfolio

Welcome to my Portfolio, where you can explore a range of projects I've had the pleasure of working on. From design and development to writing and creative collaborations, this space highlights my work and showcases the skills and passions that drive me.

Gradpass

This is a mobile web app that uses some creative methods to function as a working mobile app for iOS and Android. It was designed for our school to easily view grades from Echo (our equivalent of Google Classroom). However, we stopped using it in the 2024-2025 school year, so the project was halted.

Parking Pass

This was a failed project; however, the effort I put into the web portion was too good not to include in this portfolio. It was intended to be a parking pass website where students could obtain and easily manage their parking passes, while administrators could also manage all students, making the entire ecosystem more efficient.

Human Behaviors

This was a psychology project I had to complete, and I chose to present it by creating a website. The project focused on human behavior and explored everything related to it in depth. The site was also based on nature.com.
